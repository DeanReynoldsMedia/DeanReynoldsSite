Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from a one-time consultation?

A consultation gives you advice for one specific issue. This 12-week program provides comprehensive training, ongoing support, accountability, and implementation across all aspects of your music career. You're building a complete business foundation, not just getting answers to one question.

When do sessions happen?

Sessions are scheduled based on mutual availability. I offer evening and weekend slots to accommodate artists with day jobs. We'll find a consistent weekly time that works for both of us.

What if I miss a session?

Life happens! You can reschedule with 24-hour notice. Sessions missed without notice are forfeited, but we'll always work to accommodate genuine emergencies.

Do I need to have music released already?

No! This program works whether you're planning your first release or you've been releasing music for years. We'll meet you where you are and build from there.

What genres do you work with?

While my background is in gospel and R&B, the music business principles apply across all genres. I've coached artists in hip-hop, pop, rock, jazz, and more.

Is there a refund policy?

Due to the personalized nature of 1-on-1 coaching, payments are non-refundable once the program begins. However, I'm committed to your success and will work with you to ensure you get maximum value.

Can I upgrade my package mid-program?

Yes! You can upgrade from Standard to Premium or VIP at any time. You'll pay the difference and gain immediate access to upgraded benefits.
